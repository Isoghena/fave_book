The Duke and I


"The Duke and I" is a historical romance novel written by Julia Quinn. Set in the Regency era in London, the story follows the lives of Simon Basset, the Duke of Hastings, and Daphne Bridgerton, the eldest Bridgerton daughter. Daphne and Simon enter into a fake courtship arrangement with the aim of attracting other potential suitors while hiding their own personal motives. As their pretend relationship evolves, they find themselves developing genuine feelings for each other, leading to a complex and emotional journey of love, secrets, and societal expectations. The novel masterfully weaves together humor, passion, and social intrigue, capturing the essence of Regency England while exploring themes of family, love, and identity. "The Duke and I" is the first book in the Bridgerton series, which has gained widespread popularity and was adapted into a successful television series by Netflix.
